fix: force desktop child shutdown after window close

When the desktop window closes on Windows, supervised child processes can
linger in the background and keep the gateway port occupied. This change
tracks the child exec.Cmd handles in runServe and adds a bounded wait on
shutdown before force-killing any child that ignores context cancellation.

This keeps the desktop app from appearing closed while bifrost, qdrant, or
the indexer are still alive and blocking relaunch.
